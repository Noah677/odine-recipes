Ingredient List

  • • Chicken (boneless, skinless, cut into pieces)
  • • Olive oil
  • • Lemon juice
  • • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • • Paprika
  • • Dried oregano
  • • Salt
  • • Black pepper
  • • Red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, dried oregano, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss them in the marinade until well coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking sheet lined with foil or parchment paper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Serve the delicious baked chicken with your favorite sides and enjoy!
