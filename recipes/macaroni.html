Ingredient List

  • • Macaroni pasta
  • • Water
  • • Salt
  • • Olive oil
  • • Butter
  • • All-purpose flour
  • • Milk
  • • Cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • • Ground black pepper
  • • Bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the all-purpose flour and cook for a minute or two to make a roux.
  3. Gradually add the milk to the roux, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.
  4. Lower the heat and add the shredded cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese to the sauce. Stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy. Season with ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce until the pasta is evenly coated.
  7. Grease a baking dish with olive oil and transfer the macaroni mixture into the dish.
  8. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top of the macaroni for a crispy crust.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the macaroni is heated through.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  11. Enjoy your delicious and creamy homemade macaroni!
