Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the 1/4 cup of butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for about a minute until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Slowly add the milk to the flour mixture, stirring constantly, until the sauce is thickened.
  4. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Combine the cheese sauce with the cooked macaroni.
  6. Melt the 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet and add the bread crumbs. Stir until the bread crumbs are coated with butter.
  7. Sprinkle the buttered bread crumbs and grated Parmesan cheese over the macaroni.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes or until the top is golden and crispy.
  9. Sprinkle with paprika before serving.
